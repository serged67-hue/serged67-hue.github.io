Seven tools. One obsession.

Every way your Apple Music library can break, MusicLibrarian can fix.

Duplicates

Stop listening to the same song twice.

MusicLibrarian finds exact copies, near-matches, and quality variants hiding in your library. Its smart auto-selection keeps the best version of every track — highest bitrate, best metadata, original artwork. Delete the rest in one click.

iCloud Health

Find every broken track before it ruins your playlist.

Unavailable songs. DRM-locked files. Matched tracks that silently degraded to lower quality. MusicLibrarian scans your entire iCloud Music Library and surfaces every problem, categorized by severity — so you know exactly what to fix first.

Lossless

Hear your music the way it was recorded.

Thousands of your Apple Music tracks may still be streaming in compressed AAC when lossless versions exist. MusicLibrarian identifies every upgradeable track and batch-processes your entire library to full ALAC quality. No manual searching. No track-by-track work.

Ratings that actually mean something.

Inherited “grey” ratings silently break your smart playlists. Find them all and reset them so your ratings reflect your actual taste.

Know when two tracks are truly identical.

Compare International Standard Recording Codes to tell real duplicates from different recordings of the same song. Studio version vs. live version? Now you'll know.

Catch tracks pretending to be something else.

When metadata lies, audio doesn't. MusicLibrarian identifies the actual song in each file and flags mismatches — corrupted tags, wrong titles, swapped tracks.

Every album deserves its cover.

Find missing and low-resolution artwork across your library. Pull high-res covers from the Apple Music catalog and apply them in one click.
