Privacy Policy

AskAI Bilingual — Bilingual AI Voice Assistant

Last updated: April 14, 2026

TheVaultDJ ("we", "our", "us") operates the AskAI Bilingual mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

  • Email address — used only to create and authenticate your account.
  • Messages you send — transmitted to our secure backend for AI processing. Messages are not stored on our servers after processing.
  • Conversation history — stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to it.
  • Subscription status — managed by Apple through In-App Purchases. We only receive a verification token.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or files
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely to authenticate your account and manage your subscription. Your messages are sent to our backend server (hosted on Railway.app) to generate AI responses. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services:

  • AI provider — to generate AI responses.
  • Railway.app — backend hosting. See Railway's Privacy Policy.
  • DuckDuckGo — used for optional web search. No user data is sent. See DuckDuckGo's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — for In-App Purchases and authentication.

5. Data Storage and Security

All communication between the App and our backend is encrypted via HTTPS. Your account password is hashed using industry-standard bcrypt. Conversation history is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account email as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time by emailing support@thevaultdj.com. Upon request, all account data will be deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@thevaultdj.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
