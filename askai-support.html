Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use AskAI Bilingual?

Simply launch the app, create an account, and start chatting — by voice or text. Tap the microphone button to speak, and tap the 🇫🇷/🇬🇧 flag next to it to switch between French and English voice recognition.

What languages are supported?

AskAI Bilingual supports both English and French. The app automatically detects the language of your message and responds in the same language.

How many free messages do I get?

Free accounts receive 20 messages per day. The counter resets at midnight. Upgrade to Pro ($4.99/month) for unlimited messages.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Pro subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select AskAI Bilingual and tap Cancel

Is the web search feature accurate?

Web search results come from DuckDuckGo and are fed into the AI responses for real-time information about weather, prices, news, and more. Enable it in Settings.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations are stored locally on your device. We only send messages to our secure backend for AI processing. We never share or sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app is not responding / crashes

Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're running the latest version
  • If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version
